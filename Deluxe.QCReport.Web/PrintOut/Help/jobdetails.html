Job Details

In your browser enter a Job Number in the JOB NUMBER field for a job that already exists. (Our example is 11111). Then press the SELECT button. Next in the Tree Frame (the blue area) You will see Job#:11111. Press the + button next to it. It will display the QC# in a tree like pattern that branches downward as in the example 1A.

Next click on the number "11111" in the Tree Frame (blue area). the details for JoB 11111 will appear in the right frame. Selecting the Job Number will display the Job details including a list og the QC Numbers. From here you can ADD or DELETE QC Numbers, as in example 1B
